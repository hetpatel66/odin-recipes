Masala Chai

Easy and Authentic Indian Masala Chai

Wake your mind and body up with a warm cup of Masala Chai!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, chopped
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 1/2 cinnamon stick
  • 5 pods green cardomom pods, crushed
  • 1 pod black cardamom, split open
  • 2 black tea bags
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Add ginger, cloves, cinnamon stick, green cardamom, and black cardamom.
  3. Simmer until flavors infuse, about 5 minutes
  4. Add tea bags; simmer for 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Add milk and sugar; bring back to a boil. Strain tea into mugs.
